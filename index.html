VANTAGE. What's the point?

Recognise this? We know that your gaming table gets messy. Sheets overlap, maps smudge, dice can knock over game pieces, and drinks spillages can ruin your game.

We can solve this.

VANTAGE. is a raised gaming platform designed to elevate the surface of gameplay to give you the advantage of an eye-level vantage. We make roleplay feel real by immersing the player in the game, allowing you to see the board from your character's point of view.

Raise the stakes, raise your board.
